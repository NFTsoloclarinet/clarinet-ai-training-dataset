Dataset Overview

Premium Monophonic
Audio Corpus

A high-fidelity, monophonic audio collection specifically engineered for Machine Learning and Musical AI Research. This dataset provides pure, isolated clarinet performances — removing the complexity of accompaniment to allow for precise feature extraction and model training.

Each recording captures the full expressive range of the solo clarinet: vibrato control, dynamic articulation, legato phrasing, and the subtle timbral transitions unique to the instrument. This is not a sample library — it is a structured acoustic corpus for generative AI development.

The repertoire spans five centuries of public domain classical music — Baroque to Romantic — providing extraordinary stylistic variety within a single timbral context, ideal for training models that need to learn instrument-level expressiveness.

Research Applications

Designed for
Frontier AI

◈
Instrument Modeling
High-accuracy clarinet timbre synthesis. The pure monophonic signal provides ideal ground truth for neural instrument modeling and physical modeling research.
◈
Expressive Performance Generation
Training generative models on human-like phrasing, vibrato control, articulation, and dynamic shaping. Capture what makes performance alive — not just correct.
◈
Audio Source Separation
Clean monophonic recordings provide ideal "ground truth" reference signals for training source separation and instrument isolation algorithms.
◈
MIDI-to-Audio Synthesis
Bridging the gap between symbolic and acoustic music. This corpus trains models to produce expressive, human-quality audio from MIDI representations.
◈
Diffusion Model Training
Consistent acoustic texture across 114 files provides the stable spectral signature diffusion models need to learn high-quality instrument generation.
◈
GAN Audio Research
Diverse stylistic range — from Baroque precision to Romantic legato — within a single instrument enables robust GAN discriminator training across musical contexts.
◈
Style Transfer
Fourteen albums covering radically different articulation styles — staccato tangos, fluid romances, dramatic opera — ideal for musical style transfer research.
◈
Pitch & Feature Extraction
Monophonic recordings eliminate polyphonic ambiguity — providing clean F0 tracking, spectral envelope extraction, and onset detection ground truth.
