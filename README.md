# Clarinet AI Training Dataset | Romualdo Barone

## Solo Clarinet Classical Recordings Dataset for AI Training

### Academic DOI
This dataset is archived on Figshare with a permanent DOI:

https://doi.org/10.6084/m9.figshare.31702483

Professional solo clarinet recordings for AI training — 14 albums available.

---

## Dataset Content
The clarinet recordings include:

- Long tones and sustained notes
- Scales and interval patterns
- Articulation examples
- Dynamic variations
- Expressive musical phrases
- Complete solo clarinet pieces

---

## Possible Uses

- AI training datasets
- Machine learning research
- Sound recognition systems
- Generative music models
- Audio analysis and music technology research
